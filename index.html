Biography

Ruihui Zhao received his B.S. and M.S. degrees at UESTC and Waseda University. He joined Tencent Jarvis Lab as a senior researcher in early 2018. He has several papers accepted by ACL, WWW, AAAI, NAACL, IJCNN, TOIT, IEEE WCSP, etc. His research and projects mainly focus on NLP and information security.

We are looking for FTE & Research intern who are interested in Healthcare AI. Please send your CV to me if you want to join us.

Research Interests

  • Question Answering: Text Matching, Taxonomy Construction and Expansion, Question Generation, etc.
  • Natural Language Processing: Medical Pre-training Language Modeling, Knowledge Distillation
  • Machine Learning Techniques: Time Series Anomaly Detection, Graph Embeddings, Model Interpretability, etc.
  • Information Security: Federated Learning, Searchable Encryption
